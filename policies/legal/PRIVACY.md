# Privacy Policy (Spuric.ai)

**Effective date:** 2025-12-28  
**Service:** Spuric.ai (“Spuric”, “we”, “us”)  
**Contact:** support@spuric.ai

This Privacy Policy explains how Spuric.ai collects, uses, and shares information when you use our services.

---

## 1) Information we collect

### A) Account information
When you sign up, we may collect:
- email address
- username (if provided)
- authentication/verification status

### B) Usage and technical information
We may collect:
- log data (timestamps, feature usage, errors)
- device/browser details (approximate)
- IP address (for security, fraud prevention, and analytics)

### C) Content you provide
Depending on features you use, you may provide:
- text prompts and inputs
- uploaded data (if a feature supports it)
- audio input (if/when voice features are enabled)

We use this information to provide the service and improve reliability.

### D) Payment information
Payments are processed by third-party processors (e.g., Stripe). We do **not** store full card numbers. The processor may provide us purchase metadata such as:
- payment status (success/failure)
- amount, currency
- transaction ID
- billing country/state (if available)

---

## 2) How we use information
We use information to:
- create and secure accounts
- provide features and customer support
- process purchases and deliver Credits
- prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- monitor performance, fix bugs, and improve the service
- comply with legal obligations

## 3) Sharing and disclosure
We may share information with:
- **Service providers** (hosting, analytics, error monitoring)
- **Payment processors** (to process purchases)
- **AI/voice providers** (only as needed to generate outputs when you use those features)
- **Legal / safety** disclosures (if required by law or to protect users and the platform)

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4) Data retention
We retain information as long as necessary to:
- provide the service

Retention periods may vary depending on the data type and purpose.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
